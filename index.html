Privacy Policy — Fission

Effective date: April 10, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Fission:

  • Account information: Email address and name provided via Google or Apple sign-in.
  • Task data: Tasks, projects, and notes you create within the app.
  • Usage data: App interactions collected via PostHog analytics (anonymized).
  • Subscription data: Purchase status managed via RevenueCat. We do not store payment card information.
  • Notification preferences: Reminder times you configure in the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and sync your tasks across sessions.
  • To send local notifications based on your preferences.
  • To process and verify subscription purchases.
  • To improve the app through aggregated, anonymized usage analytics.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted on cloud infrastructure. Task data is associated with your account and is not shared with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication and database
  • Anthropic Claude — AI task analysis (task text is sent to the API; not stored by Anthropic)
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • PostHog — anonymized usage analytics

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You may delete your account at any time by contacting us. All associated data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

Fission is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
